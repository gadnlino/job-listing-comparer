Job data sources

Listings are pulled from leading public job boards so your match is based on real, current openings:

  • Remotive — remote-first tech roles
  • Arbeitnow — European and global job board
  • Adzuna — broad multi-country search

Private by design

No accounts, no sign-up, and nothing is saved — your resume is used only to generate your report and isn't stored afterwards.
