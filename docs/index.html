Career Market Fit Scanner

Upload your resume PDF and compare your skills against real job postings. Get a market-fit report with career track rankings, missing skills, and study recommendations.

Upload & analyze

Extract skills from your resume PDF using a curated keyword catalog.

Real job data

Collect listings from Remotive, Arbeitnow, and optionally Adzuna.

Career tracks

Classify roles into tracks like backend/cloud, platform, AI, security, and data.

Actionable reports

Download CSV, Markdown, and PDF reports with fit scores and study recommendations.

How it works

  1. Upload a PDF resume
  2. Choose job sources and max results
  3. Review fit scores, missing skills, and career track breakdown
  4. Download your market-fit report

Job data sources

Source Auth Notes
Remotive None Public API; attribution required
Arbeitnow None Public job board API
Adzuna Optional Free API key; skipped if not configured

Good to know

  • Keyword-based matching — no semantic embeddings or LLM for scoring
  • No user accounts or saved history
  • Executive summaries may use Groq when configured on the server (structured metrics only — not raw resume text)
  • PDF quality depends on your resume having a text layer

This is an MVP built for personal use. Your resume is processed on the server during analysis.

Job data includes listings from Remotive.
